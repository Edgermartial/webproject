About Us

Our Mission

At the Single Mothers Platform, we are dedicated to empowering single mothers by providing access to essential resources and support. We understand the unique challenges faced by single mothers due to economic, social, and political barriers, and we strive to create a community where they can thrive.

Our Services

  • Financial Assistance: Helping single mothers access grants and financial aid.
  • Job Training Programs: Offering skills training to enhance employability.
  • Legal Support: Providing guidance on custody, child support, and other legal matters.
  • Social Support Groups: Creating a network of support through community gatherings.
  • Childcare Resources: Connecting mothers with affordable childcare options.
